# Privacy Policy — Routines by Pompilot

**Effective date:** 2026-04-15
**Last updated:** 2026-04-15

---

## Overview

Routines by Pompilot ("the app", "we", "us") is a routine-building and roster-management tool for competitive cheerleading coaches and organizations. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and who we share it with.

The app is invite-only. You cannot create an account without an invitation from an organization administrator.

---

## What We Collect

### Account and Profile Information
When you sign in — via Sign in with Apple or Sign in with Google — we receive from your authentication provider:
- Your name
- Your email address
- Your profile image (if one is set on your Apple or Google account)

This data is processed by **Clerk** on our behalf. See Clerk's privacy policy at [clerk.com/privacy](https://clerk.com/privacy).

### Organization and Program Data
Data you create inside the app — including organization details, team rosters, athlete names, routine formations, slide data, positions, and skill assignments — is stored on your device and synced to our servers. This data belongs to your organization and is only accessible to members you invite.

### Photos
If you upload a gym banner image, the app requests access to your photo library. The selected image is stored on your device and uploaded to our servers. We do not access photos you do not explicitly select.

### Device Identifier
On first launch, the app generates a random identifier for your device and stores it locally. This identifier is included with data sync requests so the server can track which device last modified a record and resolve conflicts. It is not tied to your name, email, or any advertising system.

### Crash Reports
If the app crashes or encounters an error, a report is sent to **Sentry** containing the error message and stack trace. These reports do not include your name, email, athlete data, or routine content. User identifiers are not attached to crash reports. See Sentry's privacy policy at [sentry.io/privacy](https://sentry.io/privacy).

### Local Preferences
Your theme preference (light/dark/system), last-used organization, and last-opened routine are stored on your device only and are never transmitted to our servers.

---

## What We Do Not Collect

- GPS or location data
- Contacts or phonebook access
- Microphone or camera (photo library only, with your permission)
- Health or fitness data
- Advertising identifiers
- Browsing history

---

## How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide the app's features:
- Authenticating your identity and managing your session
- Storing and syncing your organization's teams, athletes, and routines across your devices
- Displaying your profile and organization membership within the app
- Diagnosing crashes and errors to improve the app

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

---

## Data Sharing

| Recipient | Data Shared | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **Clerk** | Name, email, profile image, authentication events | Identity and session management |
| **Sentry** | Error messages and stack traces (no PII) | Crash reporting and diagnostics |
| **Pompilot API** | Organization, team, athlete, routine, and position data; device identifier | App functionality and cloud sync |

No other third parties receive your data. We do not share data with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics platforms.

---

## Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device using SQLite and synced to our servers over HTTPS. Local storage uses iOS-encrypted app storage. We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service.

---

## Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at the address below. Deleting your account removes your profile data from our servers. Organization data (athletes, routines, teams) is owned by your organization administrator.

---

## Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.
